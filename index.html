BIO

IVNV is a Barcelona-based DJ whose sonic identity is rooted in the depth of techno. He moves naturally across different sub-genres and energies, always reacting to the space, the time and the dancefloor.

Having refined the art of the 'extended set' in Barcelona’s more intimate dancefloors, IVNV understands the delicate balance between the patience required for a steady warm-up and the driving momentum of a peak-time slot.
